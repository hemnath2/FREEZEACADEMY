ABOUT US

"Freeze Academy of Dance" is a premier dance studio , dedicated to providing exceptional dance training in a supportive and nurturing environment. Our experienced instructors offer a wide range of dance classes for all ages and skill levels, including Hip-Hop, Bollywood, Folk,Gymnastics contemporary,and more. Whether you're a beginner looking to discover the joy of dance or a seasoned dancer striving to refine your technique, our studio offers something for everyone. Join us and experience the joy of movement at Freeze Academy of Dance.

Our Styles​

HIP HOP

Participants explore the culture and movements of hip hop, learning breaking, popping, and locking techniques

BREAKING

Breaking classes focus on mastering foundational moves such as toprock, footwork, freezes, and power moves.

BOLLYWOOD

Students master the passionate and dramatic movements of bollywood, embracing its close embrace and intricate footwork.

CINEMATIC

Cinematic dance draws inspiration from choreography seen in movies and music videos, often incorporating dramatic movements and storytelling elements.

FOLK

Folk dances represent the traditions and customs of specific cultures, often passed down through generations within communities.

GYMNASTIC

Gymnastic dance incorporates elements of gymnastics, such as flips, leaps, and tumbling, into dance routines, blending athleticism with artistic expression.

SALSA

Salsa dance is a lively partner dance originating from Latin America, characterized by rhythmic hip movements and intricate footwork.

ZUMBA

Zumba is a high-energy dance fitness program that combines Latin and international music with aerobic exercises, providing a fun and effective workout experience.

FITNESS

Fitness dance classes focus on improving physical health and stamina through dance movements and aerobic exercises, offering a dynamic and engaging way to stay fit.
